ABOUT US

La Golosa is a delightful yet sparkling Italian-Sardinian deli-shop, a fine gem nested in London Highbury/Islington, born from the love for our roots. You can choose among the most diverse sortiment of high quality Italian brands or shop some fresh goodies coming all the way from Sardinia.

OUR MUST-HAVES

COFFEE

How can you even imagine of doing something without drinking a good coffee first? We got your back, come and get our organic bio coffee!

PASTA

There is pasta and there is Pasta, we only sell the latter. Come to have a look to the big choice of Italian high quality pasta brands, and don't forget: al dente is the way!

APERITIVO

Ok, that's something you don't want to joke around! After the office, for a gather with some friends at your's, serve them some real Aperol spritz, we hand you some bottles, come over!

CHEESE

It is as cheesy as it sounds. Do you know that Sardinia has the most delicious and tasty cheeses of the world? Well, now you know, come over to taste them!

OLIVE OIL

Oil is gold, and we have a lot! We import our extravirgin olive oil from an old little farmhouse in the heart of Sardinia, that produces only 500 liters per year, contains a large amount of antioxidants and has strong anti-infiammatory properties. Bring your own bottle for a refill or buy one in store

DESSERT

Apetite but not real hunger, just enough for a litle pastry? We have gluten-free cannoli, how about that?

LASAGNE

You have always wanted to be able to make a good lasagna, but tutorials are for kids and you have not time, 'cause, hey, this is busy London, baby? Just drop by and we get you a home made Lasagna, and yes you can choose which (and how much) cheese we put in it for you!
